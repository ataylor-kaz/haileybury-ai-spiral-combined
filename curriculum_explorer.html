The Learning Journey

A progressive framework moving students from Recognition (Primary) to Systemic Architecture (Secondary).

Competency Balance

Key Stage 1-2: Consumers & Critics

Focus on identifying AI, understanding "magic" vs logic, and basic safety.

Key Stage 3: Co-Pilots & Architects

Focus on technical understanding (datasets, bias), ethical systems, and prompting.

Primary Phase (Years 2-6)

Building the foundations of recognition and critical thought.

Secondary Phase (Years 7-9)

Technical deep-dives and ethical system design.
